# Scoring Rubric

Score each category from 0 to 10, then apply the weight.

| Category | Weight | What Good Looks Like |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Yield quality | 15 | Sustainable expected return, clear source of yield, no unrealistic APY assumptions |
| Capital preservation | 20 | Low drawdown risk, clear counterparty limits, sensible position sizing |
| Liquidity | 15 | Exit window matches the user's need, shallow liquidity and lockups are penalized |
| Compliance readiness | 15 | KYC/AML/RWA requirements are known, privacy-preserving proof path is documented |
| Execution safety | 15 | Contracts/routes are known, transaction plan is bounded |
| Market timing | 10 | Entry conditions are reasonable and slippage/rate risk is controlled |
| Council consensus | 5 | Agents agree or dissent is clearly resolved |
| Memory penalty | 5 | Past mistakes are accounted for; unresolved recurring risk is penalized |

## Decision Bands

- 85-100: recommend, if all gates pass.
- 75-84: conditional recommend, list conditions.
- 60-74: watchlist, do not execute yet.
- below 60: reject.
